Summary

, here is a summary of the key points in this primer.

Considerations for Selection

  • Native Speaker
  • Social Status
  • English Fluency
  • Intelligence
  • Technical Ability
  • Reliability
  • Loyalty
  • Demographics
  • Compatibility

Training

  • Stress the importance of the interpreter's role
  • Make sure the interpreter is translating, not providing information
  • Train your interpreter in safe, low-stakes environments

Positioning

  • Position the interpreter by your side
  • If possible, position the interpreter between you and the target
  • Always look at and talk directly to the target(s)
  • Don't talk to the interpreter

Verbal Techniques

  • Keep the entire presentation as simple as possible.
  • Use short sentences and simple words (low context).
  • Avoid idiomatic English.
  • Avoid flowery language.
  • Avoid slang and colloquial expressions.
  • Don't use profanity.
