Alice and the Ice Cream

Alice was a delightful, vivacious and painfully cute 2-year old who was our neighbor from January until August. We went on many outings and adventures with Alice and her family, to include this trip to Alameda for ice cream.

Full disclosure: The Javascript used for this sequence is overwrought and suboptimized. It should not be used as a model for anything.

Gale, Wale and Me

Gale and Wale were two Thai school teachers that volunteered to accompany my Psychological Operations team and me throughout a number of villages in their immediate area while we conducted missions in their area. After the first visit, I decided that I would get a picture with both of that at every stop we made.
